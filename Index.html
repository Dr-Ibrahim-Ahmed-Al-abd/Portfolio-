Who I Am

About Me

I am a Lecturer of Adult Health and Critical Care Nursing at the Faculty of Nursing, Egyptian Chinese University, and a Member of the Sustainable Development and International Ranking Office.

With a PhD in Critical Care Nursing from Ain Shams University (2025) and over a decade of combined clinical and academic experience, I bridge the gap between high-acuity bedside practice and evidence-based nursing education.

My clinical background spans ICU, Emergency, and Military Medical environments — giving me a uniquely grounded perspective that I bring directly into the classroom, the research lab, and the examination hall.

I am an active researcher publishing in Scopus-indexed journals, with focused interests in alarm fatigue, burnout, end-of-life ethics, and evidence-based nursing practice.
